what's discord?

robotchat is hosted on discord, a text chat service.

 

Discord was originally intended for gamers (some of it's functionality is meant to coincide with game-driven activities) but is perfectly suited for practically any online community, ours included! It functions similarly to IRC and Teamspeak.

 

Discord is completely free and it's very easy to sign up! Just click any robotchat invite link and hop directly into the server.
